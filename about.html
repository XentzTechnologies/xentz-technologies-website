About Me

Hi, I’m Preston — a cybersecurity student and the creator of Xentz Technologies. I specialize in helping small businesses secure their websites by identifying vulnerabilities, hardening WordPress setups, and implementing modern security controls that stop attackers before they even try.

With hands-on experience in website security, cloud services, Linux, and threat analysis, my goal is simple: provide affordable, effective protection for businesses that don’t have a dedicated IT team.

I focus on real-world security improvements such as disabling exploited endpoints, strengthening login systems, adding security headers, enabling Cloudflare, and eliminating exposed data that attackers commonly target.

Every project I take on is approached with professionalism, attention to detail, and a mission to protect people from the growing cyber threats online.
